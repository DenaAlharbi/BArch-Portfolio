Project Overview

Location: Emirates, Abu Dhabi

Area: 450 m²

Project Definition:
This villa blends Emirati tradition with modern sustainability.
Its design centers on privacy and comfort, with separate guest and family zones.
A central courtyard enhances light, ventilation, and serenity.
Elements like perforated façades, wind catchers, and local materials connect the villa to its desert context.
More than a house, it’s a space that breathes, belongs, and reflects its cultural identity.
